    DORABABU KOTHARU

    DIRECTOR

    Meet Our Founder: Mr. Dorababu
    Mr. Dorababu, the visionary behind Kotharu Ventures Pvt. Ltd., has an extensive and diverse background that spans multiple industries and leadership roles over the past three decades. His career began in the tobacco industry (1991-1994), where he focused on adding value to agricultural waste. From 1994 to 2004, he successfully transitioned into shrimp hatchery and aquaculture, helping to foster sustainable practices in the field. His leadership capabilities were further recognized when he served as a trustee at Visakhapatnam Port (2004-2007), one of the largest government-operated ports in India. In 2007, Mr. Kotharu entered public service and was elected Deputy Mayor of Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation, a position he held until 2011. After his tenure as Deputy Mayor, he returned to his roots in aquaculture (2011-2016), before becoming actively involved in State politics (2016-2024). During this period, he served as the State Organizing Secretary for the Telugu Desam Party. Mr. Dorababu’s leadership, dedication, and experience across industries and public service have been instrumental in the growth and vision of Kotharu Ventures Pvt. Ltd.
